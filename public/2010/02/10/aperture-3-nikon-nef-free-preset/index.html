Aperture 3 - Nikon NEF Free Preset

Feb 10th, 2010 | Comments

One of our faithful friends emailed me regarding Aperture 3 vs NX2 RAW conversion “issues”. I thought I covered this enough times before but… Here is a Nikon D50 NEF preset that happens to “match” what NX2 does with our friends camera settings - which NOTHING else reads by default. You can get to the same place about 1000 different ways. I just chose to do it this way. In reality if I were doing this over again for all my stuff (I will) I would use the curves to do all of the stuff instead of the Exposure block - maybe. I actually need more image samples to get this right as I do not have a D50 but I thought I would put this out there for the gillions of new Aperture 3 users that will be scratching their heads and asking - Hey how come Aperture’s RAW conversion for NEF files doesn’t look “good”. Here it is and it doesn’t cost anything - if you don’t like it - tweak it to your liking.
